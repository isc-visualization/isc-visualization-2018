A Glance at Global Gender Gap Index

This document is intended to let readers read through the Gender Gap Index report without having to read hundreds of pages.
Please refer to the charts and the description below to know how to browse through the massive information. Enjoy!

Select country:

Time Series Line Graph

  • This chart shows chronological data of the global index & the subordinate four indexes.
  • Choose the country you want to view the chart of. The chart will be updated right after you select from the dropdown menu.
  • To check the detailed figure of each point, put your mouse over the point. You will be able to see all five figures of the very year you are checking.
  • To learn which color indicates the five indexes (including the global index), please refer to the legend on the top of the chart.
  • FYI, the indexes are as following: global index, economic index, educational index, health index and political index.

GGG Distribution by Index

  • The charts above show you the distribution of global countries by index. The color index is same as above.
  • To view data from another year, please select the year from the drop down menu.
  • Just as the line chart explained above, you can check the specific figures by hovering your mouse above each point.
  • In case of charts that go over the highest limit of 1, it's because too many countries are concentrated near the highest number!
